ABOUT US
Marmalato Cosmetics is one of the fastest growing beauty brands in the world. Having launched three years ago, Marmalato Cosmetics was put on the makeup map, by Kylie’s infamous "Lip Kit," consisting of a matching liquid lipstick and lip liner to create the perfect Kylie pout. Kylie’s never-before-seen concept disrupted the market and changed the face of the beauty industry forever. Kylie has since gone on to expand the collection into a full range of color cosmetics, including eyeshadow palettes, concealers, highlighters, blushes, accessories and more, as well as becoming one of the most renowned and coveted cosmetics brands of 2018.

Kylie has worked hard to create a line of products that she feels captures the Kylie Jenner aesthetic. She is currently working with our labs to release some exciting new things in the near future. Follow us on social media to stay up to date with our latest announcements and releases- We promise, you won’t want to miss it!
